Mission

The mission of The Restorative Psych Behavioral health LLC is to offer high quality, safe, timely, cost-effective, and patient-centered psychiatric services for patients with mental illnesses and their families, without any discrimination to gender, race, ethnicity, social class, creed or religion.

Vision

To be the provider of choice in New Jersey and beyond in providing high quality psychiatric services and addiction treatment for patients with psychiatric/mental illnesses spectrum and addictive behaviors.
